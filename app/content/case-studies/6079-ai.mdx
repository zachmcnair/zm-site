6079 AI was a social campaign to raise awareness of decentralized AI heavily influenced by the book 1984. We designed a social game using X that gave people the chance to gain XP for sharing posts and running various missions. In Season Two, we introduced a custom currency to be used for purchasing limited edition swag.
